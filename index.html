    • 🐈 Information on different breeds 🐈

    There are hundreds of different cat breeds, and each breed has different behaviors and tendencies that appeal to different types of people!
    There are breeds that are very affectionate, and cuddley while there are also breeds that are more independent and playful!
    Furthermore, there are also certian breeds that are hyperallergenic if you are allergic to cats.

    🐈‍ Here are some different cat breeds 🐈

  • Siberian: long haired, very affectionate, and hyperallergenic.
  • Bengal: short haired, more independent, very athletic and playful.
  • Maine Coon: long haired, very affectionate, loves outdoors.)
  • Russian Blue: short haired, independent, playful.
  • Ragdoll: long haired, extremely affectionate, cuddley and lazy.
  • Egyptian Mao: short haired, very agile, and playful.
