Why is Chicago the BEST Musical

(In my opinion)

Chicago is a 1975 American musical with music by John Kander, lyrics by Fred Ebb, and book by Ebb and Bob Fosse. Set in Chicago in the jazz age, the musical is based on a 1926 play of the same title by Maurine Dallas Watkins about actual criminals and crimes on which she reported. (from Wikipedia)

why is it the best? because...
  • Timeless storyline
  • Iconic Music and Songs
  • Amazing Choreography
  • Use Satirical edge
  • Have been runing on Broadway since 1996
  • Won multiple Tonys
  • Memorable Characters
